# Review: Glossary of Terms

**Light review**

The glossary is readable and pitched at a broad audience, which suits a public strategy document. It does useful work in explaining infrastructure and commercial terms that might otherwise be opaque to non-specialist readers.

## Notable observations

- **Key strategy terms are missing.** `AI`, `AI Stack`, and `Fair Work` are all central to the document but are not defined here.
- **The glossary leans toward infrastructure and business language.** That supports the later stack sections, but it leaves governance and public-interest concepts less well explained than they could be.
- **Some definitions are looser than others.** A few entries are precise and functional, while others read more like general descriptions, so the level of specificity is uneven.
- **No obvious misleading definitions stand out.** For review purposes, the glossary is good enough to support understanding of the main text even if it is not fully comprehensive.
